Recipes for stew

Description

This recipes is for beef stew

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil or any oil of your choice
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can (15 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (15 ounces) tomato sauce
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add onion and cook until softened.
  3. Stir in garlic, diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, beef broth,bay leaf, oregano, and basil.
  4. Bring to a simmer, reduce heat, and cook for 20-30 minutes, or until flavors have combined.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
