Vegetable soup recipes

Description

This is vegetable soup made with ugu (pumpkin leaves)

Ingredients:

  • 1 bunch ugu leaves
  • 1 pound beef or chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups water

Instructions:

  1. Wash the ugu leaves thoroughly and set aside.
  2. In a pot, cook the beef or chicken until tender.
  3. Add the onion and garlic, and cook for a few minutes more.
  4. Stir in the ground pepper, salt
  5. Add the water and bring to a boil.
  6. Reduce heat and simmer for a few minutes.
  7. Add the ugu leaves and cook for a few minutes more, until the leaves are wilted and your soup is set.
