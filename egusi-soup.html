Egusi soup recipes

Description

This recipes is for egusi-soup

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup of ground egusi seed
  • 1 Kg of meat
  • 2 Onion
  • 3 Tablespoon palm oil
  • 2 Teaspoon of dried pepper or any pepper or your chioce
  • 1 Teaspoon of salt or according to your taste
  • 2 Knorr cubes
  • 1 Bunch of vegetables of your chioce
  • 1 Cup of water
  • 3 Piece of smoked fish

Instructions

  1. In a port steam your meat with onion, knorr cube and salt for at least 10-15 and add your smoked fish leave to cook for another 15-20 minutes
  2. Add the ground egusi, dried pepper salt and cube again if needed and some water
  3. Allow to cook for 5 minutes then reduce the heat and let it simmer until the egusi is cooked and the sauce thickens
  4. Add the vegetables and simmer for few more minutes until the vegetables are wilted
  5. Taste to see if it require more seasoning, and your egusi soup is ready to be saved with any swallow of your chioce
