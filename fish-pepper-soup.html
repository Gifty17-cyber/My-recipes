Recipes for fish pepper soup

Description

This recipes is for cat fish pepper soup

Ingredients:

  1. 1 pound fish, cut into pieces
  2. 1 onion, chopped
  3. 2 cloves garlic, minced
  4. 2 scotch bonnet peppers, chopped
  5. 1 teaspoon ground pepper
  6. 1 teaspoon salt
  7. 1/2 teaspoon curry powder
  8. 1/4 teaspoon thyme
  9. 2 cups water

Instructions:

  • Wash the fish thoroughly and set aside.
  • In a pot, cook the fish until tender.
  • Add the onion, garlic, and scotch bonnet peppers, and cook for a few minutes more.
  • Stir in the ground pepper, salt, broth powder, curry powder, and thyme.
  • Add the water and bring to a boil.
  • Reduce heat and simmer for a few minutes.
  • You have your pepper soup ready to be sarved.
